Campaign Activities & Reach

The Swachhta Pakhwada 2020 campaign was conducted entirely online due to pandemic restrictions, yet achieved remarkable participation through creative digital engagement strategies:

Student Engagement Activities

  • Essay Writing Competitions: Students submitted essays on cleanliness, sanitation, and environmental responsibility, reflecting on personal and community hygiene practices
  • Painting & Art Activities: Digital art submissions depicting clean environments, waste management, and public health themes, encouraging creative expression around cleanliness
  • Photography Challenges: Student-led documentation of cleanliness initiatives in their homes and neighborhoods, fostering observational and visual storytelling skills
  • Poster Design Competitions: Creation of awareness posters promoting Swachhta (cleanliness) messages for social media and community distribution
  • Digital Awareness Sessions: Online webinars, interactive sessions, and educational content distribution to schools across multiple regions

Deliverables & Responsibilities

Visual Design & Campaign Management

  • Visual Graphics Campaigns: End-to-end design of campaign assets including infographics, social media posts, posters, banners, and digital collateral for COVID-19 awareness and Swachhta initiatives
  • Annual Report Design: Layout design, infographic creation, data visualization, and editorial design for ONGC Foundation's comprehensive annual CSR reports
  • Campaign Coordination: Managed digital distribution channels, coordinated with schools and partners, and facilitated online student participation across multiple activity streams
  • Branding & Identity: Maintained ONGC Foundation's visual identity across all campaign materials while creating engaging, accessible content for diverse audiences
  • Digital Content Strategy: Developed visual storytelling frameworks that translated foundation activities into compelling narratives for stakeholders, beneficiaries, and the public
  • Impact Documentation: Visual documentation of campaign outcomes, student engagement metrics, and program reach for internal and external reporting

Impact & Outcomes

The visual campaigns and awareness initiatives successfully engaged thousands of students during a challenging period, demonstrating the power of digital design and communication in maintaining community connections and promoting public health awareness. The annual reports provided transparent, visually compelling documentation of ONGC Foundation's social impact, serving as accountability tools and stakeholder communication vehicles.

The Swachhta Pakhwada 2020 campaign, despite being conducted entirely online, achieved significant participation and reinforced cleanliness values among young audiences, contributing to broader public health goals during the pandemic.
